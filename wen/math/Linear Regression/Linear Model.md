## linear model

Definition

if you have $(x_{1},y_{1}),(x_{2},y_{2}),...(x_{n},y_{n})$ ,these data can :
$$
y = \beta_{0} + \beta_{1}x +\epsilon
$$


Suppose $E(\epsilon)=0, Var(\epsilon)=\sigma^{2}$.

So:
$$
E(y|x) = \beta_{0}+\beta_{1}x
$$

$$
Var(y|x)=\sigma^{2}
$$

$$
\hat{y}=\hat{\beta_{0}} + \hat{\beta_{1}}x
$$
