For Example:

−kx=mdvdt

if k/m=1, Get:

−x=dvdt

Init constant:when t=0, x=1,v=0.

get a ε=0.01s, we can write:

x(t+ε)=x(t)+ε∗v(t)

v(t+ε)=v(t)+ε∗a(t)

we know:

a(t)=dvdt=−x(t)

So:

v(t+ε)=v(t)−ε∗x(t)
